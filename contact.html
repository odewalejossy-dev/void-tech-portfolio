Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Most UI/UX design projects take 2-6 weeks, while graphics design can be 1-3 weeks. We'll provide a specific estimate after discussing your needs.

Do you offer revision rounds?

Yes! We include multiple revision rounds in our packages. We want to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final deliverables.

What's your pricing structure?

We offer project-based pricing tailored to your specific needs. We'll discuss your budget and requirements to create a customized quote.

Can you work with existing designs?

Absolutely! We can enhance, redesign, or improve existing work. We'll analyze your current design and suggest improvements.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, we're happy to sign NDAs to protect your confidential information and project details.

What's your design process like?

We follow a comprehensive process: Discovery → Strategy → Design → Prototyping → Refinement → Delivery. We keep you involved every step of the way.
